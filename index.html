Hello there! My name is Fadhil, a first year PhD student at College of Information and Computer Science, University of Massachusetts Amherst. I have broad interests in Distributed Systems, NVMe Storage (SSD), Networked Systems, and also Machine Learning.

Currently I am working to make privacy-preserving distributed systems with Dr. Arun Venkataramani in Advanced Networked Systems Research Group. I was very fortunate to worked with Haryadi S. Gunawi and Huaicheng Li from UCARE Lab, University of Chicago.

I received BSc degree in Informatics/Computer Science from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB), Indonesia, with cum laude distinction. During my undergraduate study there, I spent my semester break doing internships in several tech companies, such as Tokopedia, Bukalapak R&D, and Suitmedia. Previously I also worked as a Software Engineer in Ruangguru.
